Brown Sugar Salmon

The Best Flavor

There is nothing more well loved than a tasty plate of salmon in my house. But the one above all is this simple but very tasty recipe. The flavor is full bodied and although sweet isnt overpowering. Its safe to say ive turned salmon haters into fenatics over night. The salmon in the end will easily pull apart and melt into your mouth. This recipe is not one to pass up on.
